Support

We offer funding both to individual researchers and to research centres across Scotland in order to encourage and support our long-term goal of developing effective treatments and improving survival rates of those suffering for pancreatic cancer in Scotland.
A major focus of Pancreatic Cancer Scotland is to improve the standard of healthcare available to patients with pancreatic cancer across Scotland.
This covers a number of activities:
  • Improving education of what constitutes best practice;
  • Encouraging early diagnosis;
  • Encouraging the standardization of care practice in Scotland;
  • Promoting the best routes by which patients are referred to specialists for further investigation and diagnosis;
  • Improving the availability of new and innovative treatments; and
  • Facilitating participation in cancer drug and treatment trials.
